Lecturer in charge for three academic years (A.Y. 2019/20 – 2021/22), M.Sc. in Aerospace Engineering — 6 ECTS, 48 hours, scientific sector ING-IND/06 (Fluid Dynamics).

Hands-on laboratory course on the practice of computational fluid dynamics: setting up, running and critically assessing CFD simulations of aerospace-relevant flows, from meshing and boundary conditions to turbulence modeling and validation against reference data.

I introduced this course at the Department of Industrial Engineering in A.Y. 2019/20, authoring its program, contents and the supporting lecture notes.
