Lecturer in charge since A.Y. 2025/26 (second semester), M.Sc. in Aerospace Engineering — Aeronautics curriculum. 9 ECTS, 72 hours, scientific sector ING-IND/07 (Aerospace Propulsion).

Course on the principles and design of aircraft propulsion systems: thermodynamic cycles of gas turbines, turbofan/turbojet architectures, component aerothermodynamics (intakes, compressors, combustors, turbines, nozzles) and current trends toward sustainable aviation propulsion.
