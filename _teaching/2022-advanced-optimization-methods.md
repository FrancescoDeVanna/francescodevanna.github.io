Lecturer in charge for three academic years (A.Y. 2022/23 – 2024/25), M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering — 6 ECTS (48 h) in 2022/23, then 5 ECTS (40 h); scientific sector ING-IND/08 (Fluid Machinery).

Graduate course on optimization techniques applied to the thermo-fluid dynamics of machines: gradient-based and evolutionary methods, surrogate modeling and CFD-driven design workflows for turbomachinery and energy systems.
