## Awards & honors

- **2026 — Aerospace Travel Award**, conferred by the journal *Aerospace* (MDPI) and assigned by the Editor-in-Chief, Prof. Konstantinos Kontis.
- **2026 — Journal Quality selection, ASME Turbo Expo 2026**: the paper *Compressible Coriolis and Centrifugal Effects in Aeroengine-Like Rotating Boundary Layer Flows* (Regazzo, De Vanna, Benini) was selected by the conference editorial committee for submission to the *Journal of Turbomachinery*.
- **2025 — Journal Quality selections, ETMM-15** (two papers): *Wall Cooling Effects on Entropy Production in Shock-Boundary Layer Interactions* and *Impact of Wall Temperature on Transonic Gas Turbines Vane Flow Dynamics* were both selected for extension in a Special Issue of the *International Journal of Heat and Fluid Flow*.
- **2025 — Special Issue selection, European Turbomachinery Conference**: *Scale-Resolved Modeling of Gas Turbine Vanes* selected for the conference Special Issue of the *International Journal of Turbomachinery, Propulsion and Power*.
- **2024 — Invited Cassyni video seminar**: the paper *URANOS-2.0* (Computer Physics Communications) was selected by the journal's editorial board for a video seminar on the Cassyni platform — [doi:10.52843/cassyni.l1xgy7](https://doi.org/10.52843/cassyni.l1xgy7).
- **2022 — Special Issue selection, DLES13**: *High-Reynolds compressible flows simulations with WMLES and Immersed Boundary Method* selected for the *Flow, Turbulence and Combustion* Special Issue (~25 papers chosen out of 115 presented).
